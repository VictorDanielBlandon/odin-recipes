How to Make Buttered Toast: A guide for lazy people with little motivation (like me!)

One of the most basic dishes a lazy chef must know how to prepare.

Buttered toast is a clasic in many cultures around the globe. You should really know how to do it.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of sandwich bread
  • Butter

Steps:

    The steps part assumes you have a pan, a stove and a butter knife.

  1. First, start your stove to medium heat, place your pan and let it heat for about 3 minutes.
  2. Then open the butter package, get the butter knife and add as much butter as you want on one side of the bread.
  3. After you are done adding your butter place the bread with the buttered side on the pan.
  4. Be careful, don't let it cook for too long as butter burns quickly.
  5. Once it's done, cook the other side
    6. \
  6. Repeat the process again for the other slice of bread
  7. Enjoy your bread!
